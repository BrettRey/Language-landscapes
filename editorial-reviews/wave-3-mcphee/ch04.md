## Adverbs and prepositions

### Weight
This chapter is carrying more than one chapter's load. Through the diagnostics and the treatment of *before*, it has a firm spine; after that it grows annexes: learner difficulty, semantic extension, phrasal verbs, and fronting. Trimmed, it would hit harder.
