## Conversations

### Weight
This chapter is over its fighting weight. It has one strong subject, the organization of conversation, but it keeps adding adjacent subjects until the chapter becomes a survey: action, face, power, institutions, digital media. The early spine will carry a chapter; the later annexes make it sag.
