## Analyzing vocabulary

### Weight
This chapter is a little overweight. Its true line runs from the problem of what counts as a word, through counting and frequency, to the pedagogical cost of ignoring those facts; after that, the material begins to spill into neighboring chapters. The useful burden is there, but it is partly hidden by repeated previews, late annexes, and end matter that keeps the chapter on its feet after it has already landed.

### Structural flags
1. **§8.1 / Line ~25** [RITUAL]: The reader is told the route too many times before moving. The learning objectives, the introductory list, and the preview inside "What's a word" are three versions of the same furniture.
2. **§8.2.3 / Line ~95** [LENGTH]: The affix inventories run longer than their burden warrants. The chapter needs the distinction between derivation and inflection and a few productive examples; it does not need this much shelving.
3. **§8.3.3 / Line ~270** [SEQUENCE]: "Specialized vocabulary" starts a new argument about genre-specific lexis, then leaves it almost at once. Either give that turn a real section later or keep the frequency section on its line.
4. **§8.4.3 / Line ~325** [FURNITURE]: The construction-grammar turn opens a theoretical door too late and too briefly. Nothing else in the chapter leans on it, so it reads as removable.
5. **§8.5 / Line ~339** [DECORATIVE]: "Vocabulary and syntax" is valid material, but here it is a short annex. In this form it feels borrowed from a neighboring chapter rather than earned here.
6. **§8.7 / Line ~437** [RITUAL]: The advanced exercises keep the chapter onstage after its close. In a chapter already carrying extra weight, this is easy weight to lose.

### Load-bearing
1. **§8.2.1** [EARNS IT]: It establishes that "word" is not a single stable unit. That clearing work justifies the distinctions that follow.
2. **§8.2.4** [EARNS IT]: Families, lemmas, shapes, and tokens are the chapter's measuring instruments. Later counts and frequency claims have no purchase without them.
3. **§8.3.2** [EARNS IT]: Coverage turns abstract frequency into consequence. This is where the chapter begins to matter to a teacher making choices.
4. **§8.6.2** [EARNS IT]: "The frequency blindness problem" cashes out the earlier numbers as an instructional warning. It is where the analytic spine finally reaches the book's audience.
