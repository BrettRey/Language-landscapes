## Questions and interrogatives

### Weight
This chapter is a little overweight for its main cargo. It has one clean job: separate question from interrogative and then show the three productive clause patterns. It does that, but it keeps adding endings and side chambers after the structure is already standing.

### Structural flags
1. **§10.0 / Line ~1** [RITUAL]: The chapter opens with two prefatory boxes. One orienting box is enough. The second delays the first real move.
2. **§10.1 / Line ~24** [LENGTH]: The classroom anecdote earns its place as motive, but it stays onstage too long. The transcript, the seat analogy, and the after-commentary all do the same work.
3. **§10.2 / Line ~71** [FURNITURE]: The "Terminological hygiene" box is mostly book-level housekeeping. Here, only the last sentence matters: question is semantic, interrogative is syntactic.
4. **§10.3 / Line ~94** [SEQUENCE]: "Main-clause interrogatives" is a heading without enough mass beneath it. Its taxonomic sentence can be absorbed by the opening of §10.4 and §10.5.
5. **§10.4.2 and §10.5.6 / Lines ~206 and ~445** [SEQUENCE]: Semantics and intonation arrive twice in small installments. Collected once, later, they would feel designed rather than recurrent.
6. **§10.5.3 / Line ~337** [RITUAL]: The practice box is generic exercise matter. It does not press on the difficult hinge just introduced; it mostly extends page count.
7. **§10.6 to §10.7 / Lines ~456 and ~522** [SEQUENCE]: The classroom-questions section feels like the chapter's proper landing. The phrase-structure section after it makes the chapter end, then start again.
8. **§10.8 / Line ~590** [RITUAL]: After the decision tree and the classroom application, the final summary is a third recap. The chapter has already told us what it knows.

### Load-bearing
1. **§10.2** [EARNS IT]: The distinction between question and interrogative is the hinge. Without it, the chapter is only a catalog of forms.
2. **§10.4.1** [EARNS IT]: The do-support section carries the real instructional weight. It explains the mechanism students actually miss and gives the chapter a usable diagnostic.
3. **§10.5.2-§10.5.4** [EARNS IT]: The split between subject-focus and non-subject-focus interrogatives, with inversion only in the latter, is the chapter's essential structural refinement.
4. **§10.6** [EARNS IT]: Returning to classroom questions justifies the whole enterprise for teachers. It converts syntax back into use.
