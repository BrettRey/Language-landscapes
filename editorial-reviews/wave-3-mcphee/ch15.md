## Coherence and cohesion

### Weight
This chapter runs heavy. Its spine is sound: coherence is not the same thing as cohesion, and readers do part of the connecting work themselves. But too much has been hung from that spine, so what could be one taut chapter often reads like several neighboring chapters sharing a frame.

### Structural flags
1. **§0 / Line ~7** [RITUAL]: The learning-objectives box tells the reader the chapter twice before the chapter begins. Three promises would do the work of eight.
2. **§1 / Line ~40** [SEQUENCE]: The opening example earns its place, but the detour into `were` -> `used to` -> `would` starts a second subject before the first one is secured. It belongs elsewhere or in one sentence.
3. **§1.1 / Line ~78** [LENGTH]: "What is discourse?" is broader than this chapter needs. A short definition would clear the ground; the fuller defense of discourse analysis is furniture here.
4. **§3.1 / Line ~192** [LENGTH]: The reflexive-pronoun subsection begins as cohesion and then spreads into a side essay on `myself` and emphatic `himself`. The chapter can lose that acreage without losing its line.
5. **§3.2 / Line ~282** [DECORATIVE]: The second full pass through the colored Renaissance paragraph does not earn the space the first pass already spent. One demonstration is enough.
6. **§3.5 / Line ~468** [SEQUENCE]: "Discourse markers and pragmatic particles" opens a new front: position, prosody, social meaning, rapid change. It feels like the start of another chapter inserted late in this one.
7. **§3.3, §4.3, and §5 / Line ~390, ~595, ~625** [LENGTH]: The cross-linguistic material keeps returning to the same middle-ground claim about English. The point needs one home, not several.
8. **§6 and end matter / Line ~650** [RITUAL]: Summary, short-answer questions, answer key, and essay questions restate the chapter in succession. One layer of recap is enough; the rest is bulk.

### Load-bearing
1. **§1** [EARNS IT]: The Schlich opening gives the chapter a living specimen at once and shows, before terminology arrives, that omission can bind a passage as tightly as explicit statement.
2. **§2.1** [EARNS IT]: The contrast between a coherent passage with scant cohesion and a cohesive passage without coherence carries the chapter's central distinction cleanly and memorably.
3. **§3.3** [EARNS IT]: The ellipsis material earns its keep when it reaches the classroom. The "use complete sentences" box shows why the chapter matters to teachers rather than only to analysts.
4. **§4.1-§4.2** [EARNS IT]: The bridging section is indispensable. It explains why texts cohere beyond overt markers and gives the chapter its strongest extension.
