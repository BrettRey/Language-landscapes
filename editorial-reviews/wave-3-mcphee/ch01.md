## Some key concepts, terms, and ways of thinking about English grammar

### Weight
This chapter is carrying two chapters' worth of cargo. The core load is sound: teach the reader to distrust neat definitions and work by converging tests. Around that core the chapter accumulates noun drills, a long countability excursus, verbs, auxiliaries, two glossaries, and classroom apparatus; the result is heavier than the opening needs to be.

### Structural flags
1. **§1.0 / Line ~8** [RITUAL]: The learning-objectives box announces nearly the whole book. It does not narrow the reader's attention; it advertises the chapter's sprawl.
2. **§1.1.2 / Line ~59** [LENGTH]: The Wason-card box makes the same structural point as the soup example: test a definition by hunting the failure case. Once that point is made, the box keeps talking.
3. **§1.2.1 / Line ~193** [LENGTH]: "Some examples" becomes inventory. A few strong examples of prototype and edge case would do the work; the rest is accumulation.
4. **§1.2 / Line ~265** [RITUAL]: "Find the nouns" is workbook furniture. It practices a skill, but it does not move the chapter's argument forward.
5. **§1.2.2 / Line ~273** [LENGTH]: The countability section earns a place, then overruns it. Longman, French, Russian, Bangla, Middle English, synonym pairs, and determiner behavior all make the same case. This is its own lesson.
6. **§1.3 / Line ~425** [SEQUENCE]: The glossary interrupts the chapter in mid-stride. Reference matter should not stop the narrative machinery.
7. **§1.4.1 / Line ~557** [SEQUENCE]: Auxiliary verbs arrive before the surrounding system has been built. Because the section leans on later chapters, it feels parked here rather than needed here.
8. **§1.6 / Line ~609** [RITUAL]: A second glossary followed by short-answer questions, an answer key, and essay questions turns the end of the chapter into apparatus. The chapter has already asked the reader to stop often enough.

### Load-bearing
1. **§1.1.2** [EARNS IT]: "The trouble with definitions" gives the chapter its method. Without this habit of counter-example, the rest is just terminology.
2. **§1.1.3** [EARNS IT]: The technical meaning of *phrase* does real preparatory work. Later chapters will spend this definition repeatedly.
3. **§1.2** [EARNS IT]: The opening list of noun properties is the chapter's best demonstration of category by converging evidence instead of single-rule definition.
4. **§1.4** [EARNS IT]: The verbs section matters because it answers the noun discussion with a parallel test set. It keeps the chapter from becoming a one-category seminar.
