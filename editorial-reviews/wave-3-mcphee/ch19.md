## Relative constructions

### Weight
The chapter is heavier than its main load. Its real spine is the gap-antecedent relation, the basic/focused split, and the modifier-supplement distinction; around that, it has grown annexes on rarity, ambiguity, recursion, and a full tree gallery. Cut back those annexes and the chapter would gain force.

2. **§19.2.2 / Line ~160** [SEQUENCE]: The processing-difficulty material arrives before the structural system is fully in hand. It interrupts the classification and would sit better after the chapter has finished naming its parts.
