## Coherence and cohesion

### Architecture
The chapter begins with a strong governing distinction, but it does not keep that distinction in control of the whole. Its opening example is concrete and intelligent, yet the chapter soon spreads outward into a broad survey of discourse phenomena whose sequence is only intermittently shaped by the central question of how coherence differs from cohesion. The largest structural weakness is that some of the chapter's most important explanatory principles, especially inference and bridging, arrive after long stretches devoted to the inventory of cohesive devices. What should feel like an argument therefore sometimes feels like an accretion: useful parts, not always assembled in the right order.

### Structural flags
1. **§15.1 / Line ~30** [SCAFFOLDING]: The introduction repeatedly uses the chapter's key analytic terms, especially *coherence* and *discourse analysis*, before either has been defined. A concrete opening is sensible, but the conceptual vocabulary should be established earlier so the reader knows what sort of claim the example is meant to illustrate.
2. **§15.1 / Line ~60** [ARC]: The opening passage develops two lines at once: topic chaining and inference on the one hand, tense-based narrative structuring on the other. Only the first becomes architecturally central later. The discussion of *were*, *used to*, and *would* is interesting, but because the chapter does not return to it, it feels like the beginning of a different chapter.
3. **§15.1.1 / Line ~92** [TRANSITION]: "What is discourse?" broadens the scope considerably, moving into social roles, power, rhetoric, and the general aims of discourse analysis. That material is legitimate, but the chapter needs a firmer hinge explaining why this larger detour is necessary before the narrower distinction between coherence and cohesion can proceed.
4. **§15.2.1 and §15.3.2 / Lines ~118 and ~272** [REDUNDANCY]: Lexical cohesion is introduced as part of the explanation of coherence and then reintroduced later as a major type of cohesive device, with the Renaissance passage analyzed twice. The repetition does not sufficiently build on itself, so it weakens the chapter's sense of forward motion.
5. **§15.2.1 and §15.4.2 / Lines ~137 and ~565** [GAP]: The chapter offers a useful example of a coherent passage with almost no overt cohesive marking, but it withholds the explanatory mechanism for that coherence until the later section on abductive bridging. The reader is shown the phenomenon before being given the missing middle term.
6. **§15.3.4 / Line ~461** [TRANSITION]: The move from connectives to discourse markers widens the chapter from textual linkage into turn-taking, stance, prosody, and social meaning. Because that enlargement of scope is only lightly signalled, the discourse-marker section can feel appended rather than structurally required.
7. **§15.4 and §15.5 / Lines ~534 and ~601** [PAYOFF]: Abductive bridging contains one of the chapter's deepest claims, that coherence often depends less on explicit marking than on readerly hypothesis and frame knowledge. Since this arrives late, the concluding synthesis has to summarize rather than gather and resolve an argument that has been visible from the start.
8. **Learning Objectives / Line ~6** [PAYOFF]: The objectives promise treatment of implicature and extended analysis of authentic texts, but the chapter mainly develops bridging inference and relies on brief illustrative passages. The mismatch is not severe, but the advertised architecture and the delivered one are not quite the same.

### What holds
1. **§15.2.1** [STRONG]: The paired examples of coherence without much cohesion, and cohesion without coherence, give the chapter its core distinction in a memorable and testable form. This is the chapter's clearest piece of conceptual scaffolding.
2. **§15.3** [STRONG]: Within the large survey of cohesive devices, the local ordering is mostly sound. Reference comes first, then lexical cohesion, then ellipsis and connectives, which gives the reader a workable movement from more explicit ties to looser and more context-dependent ones.
3. **§15.4** [STRONG]: The section on abductive bridging finally provides the chapter with a genuine explanatory center. It clarifies why definiteness, lexical expectation, and background knowledge matter, and it retroactively justifies the chapter's recurring insistence that what is unsaid can be structurally decisive.
