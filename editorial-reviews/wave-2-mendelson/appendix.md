## Understanding language proficiency

### Architecture
This chapter has a recognizable and mostly sound shape: it begins with classroom puzzles, reframes them as a conceptual problem, moves through the institutional simplifications of CLB and CEFR, and ends by telling teachers how to live with those simplifications without mistaking them for reality. The strongest structural move is from description to critique to use. What it never quite supplies, however, is a positive model of proficiency robust enough to answer the opening question on its own terms; the critique of frameworks becomes the chapter's center of gravity, and the definition of proficiency remains more gestured toward than fully built. The result is coherent, but slightly asymmetrical: the chapter promises "what proficiency is" and delivers more decisively "how not to misconceive it."

### Structural flags
1. **§2.1-§2.2 / Line ~32** [TRANSITION]: The chapter moves from the CLB/CEFR descriptions to "What gets lost" without explicitly showing how the losses arise from the features just described. The reader can infer the connection, but the architecture would hold more firmly if each loss were tied back to a specific kind of descriptor or institutional need.
2. **§1 / Line ~14** [SCAFFOLDING]: Fluency and pragmatic competence are introduced as major dimensions of proficiency, but after the opening they do not become organizing strands in the later discussion. Because the chapter names them early, the reader expects them to structure the critique of frameworks more fully than they do.
3. **§4 / Line ~58** [ARC]: "Using frameworks wisely" is useful and logically placed, but it shifts into a list of recommendations before the chapter has fully articulated its own alternative conception of proficiency. Advice arrives before the conceptual reconstruction is complete.
4. **§5 / Line ~72** [PAYOFF]: The conclusion resolves the institutional critique well, but it does not return to the opening classroom cases or answer the question "what is proficiency?" in a single synthesized form. A closing sentence that redefines proficiency in light of those cases would give the chapter a stronger terminal beam.

### What holds
1. **§1** [STRONG]: The opening cases do real architectural work. They establish at once that proficiency is not unitary and give the rest of the chapter a genuine problem to solve.
2. **§2** [STRONG]: Framing proficiency scales as instruments that "see like a state" gives the middle of the chapter a governing idea rather than a mere survey structure. The CLB and CEFR sections are then read as instances of a larger logic.
3. **§4** [STRONG]: The practical section does not merely append tips; it translates the critique into pedagogic consequences. That conversion from abstraction to action is the chapter's most successful structural payoff.

## List of Prepositions

### Architecture
This is not an argumentative chapter but a reference instrument, so its architecture lives or dies by orientation and retrievability. The cross-reference to the prepositions chapter gives it a conceptual home, and the alphabetical list makes practical sense once the reader knows the criteria. But the appendix assumes too much of that prior conceptual work: because CGEL's category of preposition is counterintuitive for many readers, a bare inventory needs a more explicit threshold statement about what principle of classification governs inclusion. As it stands, the list is usable, but not fully self-supporting.

### Structural flags
1. **§1 / Line ~1** [SCAFFOLDING]: "classified as prepositions in the CGEL framework" does nearly all the conceptual work, yet the appendix offers no compressed reminder of the tests or principles behind that classification. For a category that deliberately cuts against school grammar, that is too much load for a cross-reference to bear.
2. **§1 / Line ~1** [GAP]: The phrase "thorough (but necessarily incomplete)" states the list's status without stating the principle of incompleteness. The reader is told that omissions exist, but not whether omission reflects rarity, disputed status, dialect limitation, or mere space.
3. **§1 / Line ~1** [TRANSITION]: Placed immediately after an argumentative chapter on proficiency, the appendix makes an abrupt generic turn from conceptual exposition to raw lexical inventory. A brief sentence marking the change in function from argument to reference would make the appendix feel designed rather than merely appended.

### What holds
1. **§1** [STRONG]: The chapter knows it is a reference tool and behaves like one. Once the reader arrives with the earlier chapter's categories in mind, the inventory is easy to navigate and clearly subordinate to the main conceptual treatment.
2. **§1** [STRONG]: The cross-reference to the earlier chapter prevents the list from pretending to be self-justifying. That is an honest and structurally sound choice, even if the bridge could be strengthened.

## List of determinatives

### Architecture
This appendix has the same reference function as the prepositions list, but it is architecturally less stable because it briefly reopens theoretical questions it cannot itself carry. The cross-reference to the main chapter is necessary, yet the note on numerals introduces a large classificatory claim and then defers it elsewhere, which momentarily shifts the appendix from reference tool to theoretical argument. That produces a small structural wobble: the chapter cannot decide whether it is merely listing items or also defending the category's outer edges. Its usefulness is real, but its framing needs tighter control.

### Structural flags
1. **§1 / Line ~1** [SCAFFOLDING]: As with the prepositions appendix, the reader is asked to accept a contested CGEL category without even a compressed restatement of the criteria that make these items determinatives. For a reference chapter, that dependence on prior memory is heavy.
2. **§1 / Line ~3** [GAP]: The parenthetical claim that numerals also belong to the category of nouns opens a substantial classificatory issue, then immediately sends the reader to unpublished work. Architecturally, that is an argument begun in an appendix and abandoned before it can support itself.
3. **§1 / Line ~3** [ARC]: The note on numerals momentarily promises explanatory payoff, but the rest of the chapter remains a list. Either the claim should be reduced to a purely delimiting note, or the appendix needs a sentence clarifying why that theoretical complication matters here.

### What holds
1. **§1** [STRONG]: The appendix does one important thing well: it defines its scope before the list begins, so the reader knows this is intended as a practical inventory rather than a full analysis.
2. **§1** [STRONG]: The numerals note, despite the structural problem above, does at least anticipate a predictable reader question about scope. The instinct to preempt category confusion is sound; it simply needs better containment.

## Ordered list of teachable graphemes

### Architecture
This is the best-built of the three list appendices because it does more than present data: it states a pedagogic purpose, names selection criteria, and explains what kind of learner the list is for. The opening paragraphs create a clear bridge from theory to classroom use, so the table feels motivated rather than merely deposited. The one architectural uncertainty lies in the word "ordered": the appendix gives several plausible principles for order, but does not clearly rank them or state which one governs the sequence. The reader can see that frequency matters, but not quite whether frequency, reliability, or combinability has final authority.

### Structural flags
1. **§1 / Line ~1** [SCAFFOLDING]: The chapter promises an "ordered list of teachable graphemes," but the opening does not explicitly tell the reader whether the order is pedagogical sequence, corpus frequency, or some hybrid of the two. Because the appendix is prescriptive as well as descriptive, that missing statement matters.
2. **§1 / Line ~4** [GAP]: The introduction names several inclusion principles, including frequency, reliability, combinability, and likely familiarity, but it does not specify how conflicts among those principles are resolved. The table therefore arrives with a clear rationale for inclusion but a less clear rationale for rank.
3. **§1 / Line ~4** [PAYOFF]: The paragraph on omitted unreliable patterns is useful, but the chapter stops just short of telling the teacher how to operationalize the sequence once the table begins. A final orienting sentence before the table could convert the data into a more explicit teaching path.

### What holds
1. **§1** [STRONG]: The introductory framing gives this appendix a real internal logic. Unlike the other lists, it tells the reader what problem it is solving and for whom.
2. **§1** [STRONG]: The examples of early decodable words do structural work, not ornamental work: they show why the selection principles matter and prepare the reader to understand the table as a sequence for building usable combinations.
