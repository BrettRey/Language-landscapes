## Standards, Standard Englishes, and what to teach

### Architecture
This chapter has a real sequence: it first separates standard from quality, then places Standard English among social conventions, then opens the harder question of grammaticality, and only after that turns to normativity and pedagogy. The sequence is intellectually sound, but the proportions are uneven, because the long central inquiry into grammaticality overwhelms the titular question of what to teach. The result is not a shapeless chapter, but a chapter with two centers of gravity, one sociolinguistic and one grammatical. The final sections do reconnect them, though later than the chapter's title and introduction lead the reader to expect.

### Structural flags
1. **§2.2-§2.3 / Line ~99** [TRANSITION]: The chapter leaves the sociopolitical account of Standard English and opens the technical inquiry into grammaticality without fully marking why the second must follow the first. The reader needs one load-bearing sentence stating that standardness and grammaticality are separate but constantly confused judgments; that would make the shift feel necessary rather than merely sequential.
2. **§2.3 / Line ~111** [SCAFFOLDING]: The excursus on the fallacy of monosemy arrives before the chapter has firmly stated why multiple senses of *grammatical* matter to the larger argument. As placed, it briefly suspends the main inquiry instead of supporting it. The claim it serves should be stated before the box, not inferred after it.
3. **§2.3 / Line ~139** [ARC]: The section on questioning grammaticality has a good method in principle, testing one possible criterion after another, but it is not visibly partitioned as such. Meaning, rule books, processing, acceptability, dialect, and register all appear, yet the reader must supply the structure that links them. A few internal signposts would convert accumulation into argument.
4. **§2.3-§2.4 / Line ~230** [GAP]: Late in §2.3, the chapter usefully distinguishes grammaticality from acceptability, dialect difference, and register. In §2.4, however, "expectedness" and "a strongly preferred alternative" are folded back into the model of grammaticality without an explicit reconciliation. The reader is left unsure whether the distinction has been refined or quietly collapsed.
5. **§2.1, §2.2, §2.6 / Lines ~40, ~86, ~384** [REDUNDANCY]: The argument that Standard English is not inherently better than other varieties is essential, but it is restated several times in nearly the same form. Repetition here does not deepen the architecture; it delays it. One statement could establish the point, the next could explain institutional power, and a later one could show pedagogical consequence.
6. **§2.5 / Line ~350** [PAYOFF]: The title promises "what to teach," but the teaching question is largely deferred until Normativity, Audience, and the Conclusion. Because the earlier sections do so much conceptual work, the reader expects intermittent returns to classroom consequence before the final act.
7. **Learning Objectives; §2.5 / Lines ~8-16 and ~337** [PAYOFF]: The objectives promise a distinction between descriptive and prescriptive approaches to grammar, but the chapter never gives that contrast a clear structural moment of its own. Normativity approaches it obliquely, yet the reader is not shown how the familiar descriptive/prescriptive opposition fits within the chapter's larger framework.

### What holds
1. **§2.1** [STRONG]: The opening distinction between standard as quality and standard as convention is exactly the right foundation. It prevents a basic category mistake from distorting every later discussion of Standard English.
2. **§2.3 Summary -> §2.4** [STRONG]: The summary question, "what makes grammatical grammatical?", provides a genuine hinge into the model section. The model arrives not as doctrine but as an answer to a problem the chapter has earned.
3. **§2.5.1-§2.6** [STRONG]: The Audience subsection and Conclusion finally give the chapter its pedagogical center. Once the argument turns from abstract categories to audiences, targets, and accommodation, the earlier sections begin to cohere as parts of one instructional architecture.
