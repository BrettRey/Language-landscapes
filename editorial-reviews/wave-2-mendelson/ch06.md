## Syntax trees

### Architecture
This chapter has a recognizable pedagogical arc: it begins by teaching the reader how to read a single tree, widens into the conceptual language of heads and dependents, then moves into construction and finally into interpretation. The middle of the chapter is its strongest structural stretch, because the small phrase-level trees accumulate into larger ones and give the reader a concrete sense of hierarchical growth. But the chapter delays some of its rationale until after it has already asked the reader to absorb the notation, and its closing sections on function labels and terminology feel less like culmination than like appendices added after the natural end. The result is a chapter with a real shape, but one whose ending diffuses rather than completes its central argument.

### Structural flags
1. **§6.4 / Line ~105** [ARC]: The question "why trees?" is answered only after the reader has already been asked to decode one tree and absorb the solar-system metaphor. That rationale is structurally foundational, not supplementary; placed earlier, it would tell the reader why this notational system deserves the effort.
2. **§6.5.1 / Line ~135** [SCAFFOLDING]: The section on "Who helped you?" validates its analysis by invoking both an earlier taxonomy of dependents and the seven checks from a later section. A reader moving straight through this chapter is asked to rely on supporting machinery that has either not yet arrived here or is only available by looking elsewhere.
3. **§6.5.2 / Line ~175** [PAYOFF]: The announced top-down example is *I had my breakfast*, but the walkthrough abruptly builds a tree with *helped* and *you*. Because this subsection is meant to model a procedure, not merely supply an illustration, the break in continuity weakens the chapter's instructional scaffold.
4. **§6.5.2 / Line ~180** [PAYOFF]: The learning objectives promise construction by both top-down and bottom-up methods, but only the top-down method is explicitly taught as a named procedure. The later accretion from *happy* and *very* to larger phrases performs bottom-up work implicitly, but the chapter never frames it as the second half of the promised pair.
5. **§6.5.4 / Line ~315** [SCAFFOLDING]: Constituency tests arrive after the reader has already been led through several tree-building exercises. Since the chapter itself says constituent identification is crucial to drawing more complex trees, these diagnostics would do more structural work if introduced before or alongside the first substantial construction sequence.
6. **§6.8 / Line ~465** [TRANSITION]: The justification for function labels comes very late. By this point the chapter has already depended on those labels throughout, so the argument feels retrospective when it should have been orienting; and it introduces *adjunct* precisely at the moment when the chapter ought to be consolidating known terms.
7. **§6.9 / Line ~490** [ARC]: The excursus on *adverbial* opens a new polemic after the chapter has essentially reached its natural climax in disambiguation. It belongs either earlier, where category and function are first distinguished, or in a chapter devoted to terminology; here it loosens the final shape.
8. **§6.10 / Line ~520** [PAYOFF]: The summary claims the chapter prepares the reader for coordination, subordination, questions, and other advanced topics, but the body does little to stage those futures. The closing promise reaches beyond the chapter's actual instructional arc instead of crystallizing what this chapter specifically accomplished.

### What holds
1. **§6.2-§6.3** [STRONG]: The movement from one fully glossed tree to the solar-system metaphor is structurally intelligent. First the reader learns to decode the notation; then the chapter abstracts the underlying relation of head and dependent.
2. **§6.5.3-§6.6** [STRONG]: The expansion from minimal phrase trees to combined phrases and then to clause-level trees is the chapter's best piece of scaffolding. It gives the reader a persuasive sense that larger syntactic structures are built from small, reusable components.
3. **§6.7** [STRONG]: The disambiguation section supplies the chapter's clearest payoff. It shows not merely how trees are drawn, but why they matter: they make competing readings visible in a way prose description cannot.
