## Negation

### Overview
This chapter is energetic and mostly clear. At its best, it makes the history of negation feel both logical and alive; the prose has enough swing to carry a long explanation. But the chapter also has a habit of restating conclusions after already proving them, and a few jokes tip from relaxed into self-display.

Its grammar obscures the claim instead of carrying it.
