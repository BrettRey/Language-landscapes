## Some key concepts, terms, and ways of thinking about English grammar

### Overview
The chapter has real energy, and the conversational voice usually helps rather than hurts. It is strongest when it moves briskly from example to consequence and when it allows a hard point to crystallize in a clean sentence. Its main weakness is repetition: you explain the method, defend the method, and then defend it again, which slightly blunts the force of the argument.
