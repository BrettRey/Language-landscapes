## Questions and interrogatives

### Overview
This chapter is lively and mostly clear, and it is strongest when the prose trusts the example and lets the argument bite. The colloquial register usually helps, especially in the classroom sections, but it sometimes slides into a wink that feels slightly overmanaged. The tree explanations and final recap are the loosest pages; they repeat rather than deepen.

### Flags
